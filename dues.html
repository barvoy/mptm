Join Menlo Park Toastmasters!

To join us, you need to:

  1. Pay a membership fee (below)
  2. Fill the new member form and send the signed copy to our Treasurer: treasurer@menlopark.org
  3. Join our club's mailing list. This is where we send speech details and our schedule
  4. Request a name tag with your name from our Sergeant of Arms: saa@menloparktm.org
